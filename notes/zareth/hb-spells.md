Fire Syphon (3rd Level Abjuration)
Casting Time: Reaction, which you can take when you see a creature within 60 feet of you making an attack or casting a spell that is fire damage based
Range: 60 feet
Components: S
Duration: Instantaneous
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Description: When a creature casts a spell or makes an attack that would deal fire damage, and the caused effect is inside your range, you can attempt to absorb the fire damage caused by it, negating it.
If the creature casts a spell of the same level you cast this spell or lower, you negate the fire damage, if it is higher, make an ability check using your spellcasting ability. The DC equals 10 + spell level, on a success the fire damage is negated.
If the creature makes an attack or creates an area of effect that would deal fire damage, roll the save or take the hit. If you either pass the save or the attack misses you, the whole fire damage of the attack is negated.

Fiery Weapon (3rd Level Evocation)
Casting Time: Bonus action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes
Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard, Warlock, Artificer
Description: You touch a weapon and imbue it with fiery energy. Until the spell ends, it emits bright light in a 20 foot radius and dim light in an additional 20 feet. In addition, it becomes a magic weapon and weapon attacks made with it deal an extra 2d8 fire damage on a hit for the duration.
At Higher Levels: You can target 1 additional weapon for each level above 3rd.

Explosive Jumps (1st Level Transmutation)
Casting Time: Bonus action
Range: Self
Components: V, S
Duration: 1 minute
Description: Your jumping distance is tripled as you conjure fiery explosions from your feet and palms that propel you in the air.

Explosive Step (2nd Level Conjuration)
Casting Time: Bonus Action
Range: Self
Components: V
Duration: Instantaneous
Description: You conjure a fiery ball and detonate it near yourself to propel you into the air, targeting a landing space you can see up to 30 feet.

Flame Strike (4th Level Evocation)
Casting Time: Action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V,S,M (A pinch of sulphur)
Duration: Instantaneous
Description: A vertical column of brilliant fire roars down from above. Each creature in a 10-foot-radius, 40-foot-high Cylinder centered on a point within range makes a Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 Fire damage and 4d6 Radiant damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one.
At Higher levels. The Fire damage and the Radiant damage increase by 1d6 for each spell slot level above 5.

Scorching Earth (3d Level Transmutation)
Casting Time: Action 
Range: 120 feet
Components: V,S,M (piece of coal)
Duration: Instantaneous
Description: Choose a point you can see on the ground within range. A fire geyser erupts in a 20-foot cube centered on that point. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d12 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Additionally, the ground in that area becomes difficult terrain until cleared away. Each 5-foot-square portion of the area requires at least 1 minute to clear by hand.

At Higher Levels. When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the damage increases by 1d12 for each slot level above 3rd.

Fire Form (4th level evocation)
Casting Time: Action
Range: Self
Components: V,S,M (a bit of phosphorous or a firefly)
Duration: 10 minutes
Description: Thin and wispy flames wreathe your body for the duration, shedding bright light in a 10-foot radius and dim light for an additional 10 feet. You can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it.

Whenever a creature within 5 feet of you hits you with a melee attack, the shield erupts with flame. The attacker takes 2d8 fire damage. In addition, whenever you attack a creature with a melee attack, the creature takes 2d8 additional fire damage.
